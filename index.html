The Bottlenose Dolphin

The Bottlenose Dolphin is the most majestic creature to have ever lived. The Bottlenose Dolphin has been on earth for 2.4 billion years, and have remained relatively unchanged through all that time. Bottlenose Dolphins have single handedly saved 3,000,000 drowning swimmers so far in 2018, and have saved the Gulf of Mexico from three separate oil spills.

We all owe our respect to the Bottlenose Dolphin, and you will give it to them. I am serious, do not mess with us, I mean them. They are totally badass.
